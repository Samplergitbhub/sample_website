Humble Beginnings

The journey to HackerRank first began in July 2009, when computer science graduates Vivek
and Hari worked at Amazon and IBM (respectively) for about a year in Bangalore. Both were
on interview panels, inundated with endless hours of resume reviews, phone screens, and onsite
interviews. Unqualified people were making it to the onsite, while some of their most capable,
hardworking friends were passed on because of their GPA or lack of a prestigious degree.

Thats when they noticed a massive problem and opportunity in the technical recruiting system.
They quit their jobs to build InterviewStreet, a website that matched mentors with students.
Within a few weeks, hundreds of students signed up across a dozen universities. They received their
first cheque for 413.62 INR ($5.68). A copy exists in the Bangalore office and underneath, it says
“Humble Beginnings.”
